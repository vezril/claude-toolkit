# Starting a business in Canada/Quebec — the steps

End-to-end checklist (Québec.ca/REQ + CRA, as of 2026-06 — **verify current fees/thresholds/rules** on the official sites; not legal/tax advice).

## The identifier landscape (don't confuse these)
- **NEQ** — *numéro d'entreprise du Québec*, 10 digits, issued by the **Registraire des entreprises (REQ)** on Quebec registration/incorporation. Quebec identifier.
- **BN** — federal **business number**, 9 digits, issued by the **CRA** (or automatically on federal/most-provincial incorporation). Federal identifier; base for CRA program accounts (`BN + RT/RP/RC… + reference`).
- A Quebec business often has **both** a NEQ (REQ) and a BN (CRA). **Quebec does not auto-issue a BN** — you register for it separately with the CRA.

## Decision: legal form (with an accountant/lawyer)
| Form | Liability | Tax | Register where |
|------|-----------|-----|----------------|
| Sole proprietorship / *entreprise individuelle* | **Unlimited** (personal) | Personal return | REQ (if not under your own name) |
| Partnership / *société* (SENC/SEC) | Generally shared/unlimited (general partners) | Flows to partners | REQ |
| Corporation / *société par actions* | **Limited** | Separate corporate return | REQ (Quebec) **or** Corporations Canada (federal) + REQ |
| Co-op / Non-profit (OBNL) | Special | Special | REQ / federal |

## Quebec path (REQ)
1. **Pick & reserve a compliant name** (French-language + non-conflicting; check REQ).
2. **Register (immatriculer)** the business or **constitute (constituer)** the corporation with the **REQ** → receive the **NEQ**.
3. File the **ultimate-beneficiary** information (transparency rules, since 2023) and keep the registration current (annual updating declaration, usually with your QC return).
4. (If federally incorporated) **register the federal corporation with the REQ** to operate in Quebec (extra-provincial → NEQ).

## Federal path (Corporations Canada / ISED)
1. **Incorporate federally** for a fee → name protection Canada-wide.
2. On approval: **automatic BN + RC (corporation income tax) program account** from the CRA.
3. **Register with the REQ** to do business in Quebec (NEQ).
4. Add any other CRA program accounts you need (RT/RP/…).

## CRA: BN + program accounts
- Get a **BN** if you incorporate or need any CRA program account (one per legal entity; find an existing one if unsure).
- Add the accounts you need:
  - **RT** GST/HST — over the **$30,000** small-supplier threshold, or immediately for some activities (ride-share/taxi). *In Quebec, register via Revenu Québec (below).*
  - **RP** payroll — when you pay employees.
  - **RC** corporation income tax — if incorporated (auto with fed/most-provincial inc.).
  - **RZ** information returns; **RR** charity; others (RD/RE/RN/LT/RU/RG/PT) as needed. **RM import/export → now CBSA/CARM** (since Oct 2024).
- Register: CRA Business Registration Online / My Business Account, by phone, or via the incorporation process.

## The Quebec split: Revenu Québec vs CRA
- **Revenu Québec administers, in Quebec:** **GST (TPS)** *and* **QST (TVQ)** registration & remittance, **Quebec source deductions** (payroll), and **Quebec corporate income tax**.
- **CRA handles:** the **BN**, **federal** corporate income tax (RC), **federal** payroll (RP) remittances/T4s, GST/HST elsewhere in Canada.
- So a Quebec business commonly files/registers with **both**. Register for **GST + QST with Revenu Québec** at/over the **$30,000** threshold.

## Then (ongoing)
- Business **bank account** + **insurance** + **bookkeeping/accounting** from day one (separate business/personal money, especially for a corporation).
- **Permits/licences** (municipal zoning, sector/health, professional orders) per your activity.
- **Trademark** (optional, federal via CIPO) is separate from a registered business name.
- **Recurring obligations:** REQ annual updating declaration + beneficiary info; corporate minute book/resolutions (inc.); federal + Quebec tax returns; GST/QST remittances; payroll source deductions; record retention.

## Portals (verify URLs)
- Québec.ca → *Démarrer une entreprise* / Registraire des entreprises (REQ): registration & incorporation, NEQ, transparency.
- CRA → *Business registration* / *Business number and CRA program accounts*: BN + program accounts.
- Corporations Canada (ISED): federal incorporation.
- Revenu Québec: GST/QST (TPS/TVQ), source deductions, Quebec corporate tax.

Always confirm the current process/fees on these official sites or with a professional before filing.
